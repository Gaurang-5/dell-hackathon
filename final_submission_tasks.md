## Task 1: Create the Slide Deck (Assign to Teammate 1)
**What to do:**
1. Open Google Slides, PowerPoint, Canva, or Figma Slides.
2. Pick a clean, professional "Enterprise" template (light colors, minimal design, looks like a B2B SaaS pitch).
3. Open the `design_rationale_deck.md` file located in the project root.
4. Copy and paste the text from that file directly into the slides—exactly one section per slide.
5. **CRITICAL:** On the slides discussing the UI (like the "Inbox-Style Layout" or "Data Weighting"), take screenshots of our working React app and paste them onto the slides to prove it's real.
6. Export the final presentation as a PDF.

## Task 2: Format the Usability Report (Assign to Teammate 2)
**What to do:**
1. Open Microsoft Word or Google Docs.
2. Open the `usability_test_report.md` file located in the project root.
3. Copy all the text and paste it into the document.
4. Make it look like an official, professional report. Use nice heading styles, format the demographics as a clean table, and maybe add a logo at the top.
5. Export the final document as a PDF.

## Task 3: Record the Demo Video (Assign to Teammate 3 or You)
**What to do:**
1. Use Zoom, Loom, or OBS to record your screen and microphone.
2. Make sure the React app is running locally (`npm run dev`) and looks perfect in the browser (hide your bookmarks bar and make it full screen).
3. **The Script (3-5 minutes max):**
   * **0:00 - 0:30:** Introduce the problem (Opaque AI hurts trust).
   * **0:30 - 1:30:** Show the new "Inbox-Style" dashboard layout. Click between different recommendations in the left list to show how the right pane updates dynamically. Point out how clean and non-overwhelming it is.
   * **1:30 - 2:30:** Focus heavily on the "AI Analysis & Reasoning" section. Read the multi-agent steps out loud. Emphasize the "Data Weighting" bars (mention that this is our SHAP/LIME explainability implementation in plain English). Point out the yellow Limitation Banner.
   * **2:30 - 3:30:** Scroll down to the Human Controls. Show the "See Alternatives" button. Then click the "Ask Why" button and type a question in the chat to show the AI explaining itself. Finally, click "Escalate to Human Review".
   * **3:30 - 4:00:** Navigate to the "Audit Log" tab. Show how the escalated action was securely recorded. 
4. Upload the video to YouTube (Unlisted) or provide the MP4 file as required by the submission rules.
